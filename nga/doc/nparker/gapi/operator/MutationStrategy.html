Interface MutationStrategy

public interface MutationStrategy
Provides facilities for mutating a single Chromosome within a genetic algorithm. Unlike CrossoverStrategy and SelectionStrategy, we do not operate on an entire population at one time. Instead, we operate on a single Chromosome at a time. This could be extend to operate across an entire population at a given time, though this would also force a change in the main algorithm driving this application.
